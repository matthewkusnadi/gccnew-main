Sermon Series

At Grace Community Chapel Sydney, we believe in the power of God's Word to transform lives. Our sermon series are carefully crafted to dive deep into Scripture, addressing real-life challenges and inspiring spiritual growth. Each series is designed to equip you with biblical wisdom and practical insights for your faith journey. Join us as we explore God's truth together and learn how to apply it in our daily lives.

Join us for our new series, "Not Our Home," exploring 1 Peter's message of hope and perseverance as we live as strangers in a foreign land. Discover how faith shapes our journey toward our eternal home in Christ.

Past Series

Seed to Fruit

The concept of "Seed to Fruit" is beautifully illustrated in John 12:24: "Unless a kernel of wheat falls to the ground and dies, it remains only a single seed. But if it dies, it produces many seeds." This verse captures the essence of spiritual growth and transformation. Like a seed that must be buried to grow, we often need to let go of our old selves to flourish spiritually. This process, though challenging, leads to abundant life and the ability to nurture faith in others, much like a fruitful tree providing nourishment and new seeds.

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Scripture Focus: John 12, Galatians 5
